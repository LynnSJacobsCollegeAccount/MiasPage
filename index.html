Recent Projects

Safety in Atlanta

Last semester I built a data science tool that helps to identify locations in Atlanta that are most likely to be a crimes scene in the next future. This tool uses data about population about the location, business, close by as well as data about past crimes in Atlanta and their locations and on recent crimes in Atlanta to determine which areas need the most police presence to prevent crimes and which areas should be avoided by pedestrians.
